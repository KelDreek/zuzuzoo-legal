1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Zuzuzoo ("App", "we", "us", "Operator") collects, uses, discloses, and protects information when you use our App. The App is designed for young children (target: up to 4 years) and aims to minimize personal data collection. Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using the App you agree to the terms described below.

2. What data we collect

2.1. No personal data of children

The App does not intentionally collect personal information of children (for example: names, photos, voice recordings, precise location, contact lists, or other direct identifiers).

2.2. Technical and analytics data (non-personal)

We collect limited technical and analytics information to provide, maintain and improve the App. This data is anonymous or pseudonymized and may include:

  • Device information (device model, OS version, app version).
  • App usage analytics (events, session length, feature usage).
  • Crash reports and logs (collected by Bugfender or similar services).
  • Subscription status and purchase receipts handled via Apple/RevenueCat.

This information is used only to support the App and is not used to identify a child personally.

3. How we collect data

Data is collected via:

  • Built-in analytics SDKs (e.g., Firebase) for anonymous usage statistics and performance monitoring.
  • Crash reporting SDKs (e.g., Bugfender) for diagnosing app stability issues.
  • Subscription management services (e.g., RevenueCat) to manage purchase state. Actual payments are processed by Apple via the App Store.

The App does not require account registration and stores user content locally on the user's device unless explicitly exported or shared by the user.

4. Legal bases for processing (GDPR)

For EU users (including Poland) our legal bases are:

  • Performance of a contract: processing necessary to provide paid subscription services and app functionality.
  • Legitimate interests: improving the App's stability and features (analytics and crash reports), balanced against user privacy. We do not use analytics to profile children or identify individuals.
  • Where required, we will ask for consent before processing data beyond what is described here.

5. Use of collected data

  • Provide and maintain the App and subscription services.
  • Monitor, analyze and improve app performance and user experience.
  • Detect, prevent and address technical issues and crashes.
  • Manage subscriptions and verify purchase state via RevenueCat/Apple.

6. Disclosure of data to third parties

  • Analytics providers: Firebase (Google) for aggregated analytics and events.
  • Subscription management and billing: RevenueCat and Apple (App Store).
  • Crash reporting: Bugfender for crash logs and diagnostics.
  • Other service providers under contract.

7. Data retention

Analytics and crash data are retained for limited periods. Subscription metadata is kept as needed. Local app data remains on the device unless deleted by the user.

8. International transfers

Third-party services may process data outside the EEA. Transfers are used to provide services and improve the App.

9. Your rights (EU/GDPR)

  • Access to data we process about you;
  • Correction of inaccurate data;
  • Erasure where applicable;
  • Restriction or objection in certain circumstances;
  • Data portability where applicable.
